# Summary

Kitchen sponge holder that allows the sponge to drain. It's set at a 45 deg angle to the counter.  The legs are snap fit. I used PETG for the print. If you use another type of material, it may snap the legs. 
Dimensions for the sponge rest are: 80 x 100 mm. 2.4mm thick

I spent 20 minutes making it and didn't make sure the holes are perfect in the legs so a little fudging is required to get them to fit.
