# Summary

Slim double battery dispenser for AAA and AA batteries.

With this print you will use a maximum of 54 grams of material and 9 hours of print time. 

This design was an attempt to print a battery organizer with far less material than the designs available right now. Please give me a feedback as I continue to try and update the design in order to save even more material.

I have printed with the use of supports. It cost me 1 gram of support material but the peace of mind that it was going to turn out flawless was worth it.
